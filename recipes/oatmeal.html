Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a popular breakfast food; originated in Greece. Oatmeal is created by stewing oats using warm milk or water.Fruits are the usual go-to topping for oatmeals.

Ingredients

  • Water/Milk
  • Salt
  • Rolled oats
  • Sugar
  • Fruits

Steps

  1. Combine water/milk and salt in a saucepan, set to medium heat.
  2. Stir oats inside saucepan and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Turn off heat and let the oatmeal cool off for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour oatmeal in a bowl.
  5. Add sugar and fruits to the oatmeal for sweetness and fruitiness.
  6. Serve.
