French Fries

Description

French fries are a very popular snack/side dish; originated from Germany. They are deep fried potatoes cut into thin strips. They are often used with dipping sauces such as ketchup.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into thin pieces using a knife.
  2. Stir water and sugar together in a bowl.
  3. Soak potatoes in the mixture for 15 min.
  4. Remove potatoes from mixture and dry them.
  5. Turn on deep fryer and heat the vegetable oil in deep fryer to 375 degrees.
  6. Pour the potatoes into the deep fryer and fry them for 5 min.
  7. Take out the potatoes and serve.
