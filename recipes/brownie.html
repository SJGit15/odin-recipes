Brownies

Description

Brownies are a popular treat invented in the United States of America. It is a small chocolate cake with a soft texture. The brownie is a symbol of joy.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • Canola Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract in one bowl.
  2. Whisk the eggs, water, and oil in another bowl.
  3. Combine the mixtures together.
  4. Grab a baking pan and spray the inside with cooking spray
  5. Grab a piece of parchment paper and put it inside of baking pan.
  6. Pour the batter into baking pan.
  7. Put it in the oven and bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes.
